Quick Tips

  • Google yourself: See what anyone can find about you.
  • Delete old accounts: Remove profiles or blogs you no longer use.
  • Opt out of data brokers: Visit sites like Spokeo or Whitepages to request removal.
  • Limit sharing: Think before posting—once it’s out, it’s hard to remove.
